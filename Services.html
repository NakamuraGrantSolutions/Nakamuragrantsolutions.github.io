Our Services

We help organizations restructure, stabilize, and secure funding — not just write grants. Our work strengthens the systems behind your programs so your team can operate with clarity and confidence.

Organizational Restructuring for Funding Stability

We help teams redesign the internal systems that support programs, funding, and long‑term sustainability.

What this includes:

  • Program structure audits to identify friction points and duplication
  • Workflow redesign to improve clarity and reduce bottlenecks
  • Role and responsibility mapping for staff and leadership
  • Decision‑making frameworks that reduce confusion and conflict
  • Budget alignment with funder expectations and program goals
  • Multi‑year funding strategy tied to organizational capacity
  • Board communication support during restructuring
  • Implementation support to ensure changes stick

Funding & Grant Strategy

We build the systems, strategy, and structure behind successful grant seeking — far beyond proposal writing.

What this includes:

  • Funding landscape analysis and opportunity identification
  • Multi‑year grant calendar development
  • Case‑for‑support and organizational narrative development
  • Program logic models and theory‑of‑change development
  • Budget narratives and funder‑aligned financial storytelling
  • Grant readiness assessments and gap analysis
  • Grant systems setup (templates, file structure, workflows)
  • Post‑award reporting systems and compliance support
  • Partnership development for collaborative grants

Grant Proposal Development

We develop compelling, funder‑aligned proposals that clearly communicate your impact and capacity.

What this includes:

  • Full proposal drafting from concept to submission
  • Editing and narrative refinement for clarity and impact
  • Budget development and justification
  • Attachment preparation and compliance review
  • Funder‑specific tailoring and alignment
  • Review and submission support

Strategic Communication & Stakeholder Alignment

We help you communicate change, impact, and direction to the people who matter most.

What this includes:

  • Board‑ready decks and presentations
  • Funder‑ready summaries and program descriptions
  • Impact storytelling and narrative development
  • Internal communication during restructuring
  • Change‑management messaging

Implementation Support

We stay with you through the rollout — ensuring your plan becomes reality, not a document on a shelf.

What this includes:

  • Weekly or monthly advisory support
  • Staff coaching and capacity building
  • Board communication preparation
  • Real‑time troubleshooting during implementation
  • Accountability check‑ins
  • Adjustments to the plan as conditions change

Additional Support

These services can be added to any engagement or provided as standalone support.

Optional add‑ons:

  • Partnership mapping and outreach
  • Research support for grants or program development
  • Data collection setup and impact measurement frameworks
  • Drafting policies and procedures
  • Program design and refinement
  • Budget scenario planning
