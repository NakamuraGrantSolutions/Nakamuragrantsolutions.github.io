About Nakamura Grant Solutions

We work with nonprofits, social enterprises, and public‑sector teams who know something fundamental needs to change — but don’t have the bandwidth to step back, map the system, and rebuild it while the work continues.

Services

We tailor every engagement to your organization’s needs, but most projects fall into these areas.

Organizational Restructuring

We analyze your current structure, identify friction points, and redesign workflows, roles, and decision paths to support clarity, accountability, and long‑term stability.

Funding & Grant Readiness

We align your programs, budgets, and narratives so funders can clearly see the value and feasibility of your work. This includes funding strategy, case‑for‑support development, and targeted grant preparation.

Strategic Communication

We translate complex organizational work into clear, compelling messaging for boards, funders, partners, and the communities you serve — without losing nuance.

How We Work

You’re juggling active work while trying to change how that work is structured. Our process is built to be honest about constraints — time, staff capacity, and political realities inside your organization.

1
Rapid diagnostic

We start with a focused discovery: what’s working, what isn’t, and what’s non‑negotiable. You get an outside read on structure, strategy, and funding — with concrete options, not a 60‑page report.

2
Strategic restructure plan

We co‑design a realistic plan to evolve your structure, programs, and funding approach in phases. Each step is tied to specific outcomes, ownership, and timelines.

3
Support through implementation

As you roll changes out, we stay close enough to help adjust and communicate — to boards, staff, and funders — so the work doesn’t stall at the first obstacle.
